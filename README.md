# UnderRunner

When the night falls over the city of Malos Aires, the engines of the K-Drivers roar with excitement.

Under Runner is a dangerous, anything-goes illegal street race where the best and dirtiest driver's battle it out for a juicy cash prize.

Play as Violet, an ambitious young woman looking to free herself from a couple of debts.

Riding her mother's k-drive and her bat in one hand, Violet will not be afraid to face the dangers.

Fight against the trickster Gabe, a fugitive from the law, and his robot minions.

He will do everything he can to get you out of this race, from frying you with his laser beam to throwing his dangerous bombs at you!

Use all your skill to avoid obstacles at high speeds!

This is a game being developed by students from <a href="https://www.imagecampus.edu.ar/">Image Campus</a>
